What is good UX to me?

Is it true that less is more? No, less is less and that's a great thing! Good UX contains a lot of different parameters. However, these parameters do not need to be visible. Good UX boils down to what the viewer feels when looking at a website or application. It is purely subjective. You cannot please every user but you can do the right things to avoid upsetting the majority.

For me, good UX is when a website is intuitive, displaying only the necessary and is functional. Also, do not underestimate the effect of colours.
