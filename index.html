What is good UX to me?

Is it true that less is more? No, less is less. And that's a great thing! Good UX contains a lot of different parameters. However, these parameters do not need to be visible. Good UX boils down to what the viewer feel when looking at a website or application, thus, it is subjective. You cannot please every user, but you can do the right things to avoid upsetting the majority.

What do I think? Good UX is when a website is intuitive, displaying only the necessary and is functional. Also, do not underestimate the effect of colours. Do you want to discuss UX with me? Fill out the form on the bottom of this website!
